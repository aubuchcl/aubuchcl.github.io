Pair programming is the activity of pairing with someone to work through an exercise or challenge. It is based around the idea of haveing one person as a driver and one as a navigator. The two positions are agreed on between the participants and are executed as follows. The driver is responsible for leading on screen with typeing and formatting. The navigator is responsible for guiding the driver if he/she steers off course etc.
